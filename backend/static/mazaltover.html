MazalTover

This feature sends messages as you — automatically

For each monitored group, the bot watches for congratulation messages (מזל טוב, mazel tov, congrats, 🎉🎊🥳 and similar). Once a configurable number of different people send such messages within a time window, it assumes someone is being celebrated and sends your message on your behalf.

Threshold

How many unique people must say mazal tov before the bot joins in.

Time window

All those people must send their messages within this many minutes of each other.

Cooldown

After the bot sends, it won't fire again in the same group for this many hours.
